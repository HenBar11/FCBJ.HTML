and was in fact the first body to operate in an organized manner in the Betar stands since its inception.

La Familia-



The "La Familia" fan organization was founded in 2005.
In early 2006, the Beitar Jerusalem team recognized this organization as its official fan organization.

As part of its activities, the organization organizes fundraising events, as well as supporting a number of charities and volunteer organizations.
In particular, the organization supported IDF soldiers and residents of the south during military operations in the Gaza Strip.
The organization also organizes happening events for fans during the Passover and summer holidays.

This fan organization is known in the media as having an extreme right-wing orientation.
The name "La Familia" was chosen inspired by films about the Italian Mafia.
The name of the fan organization has been linked several times to incidents of violence in sports such as:
attacking fans of other teams, throwing a Molotov cocktail at the offices of the Football Association, and acts of racism.
The members of the organization and the actions attributed to them are controversial among the fans of the team.
